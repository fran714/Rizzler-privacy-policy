We’re committed to protecting your data and giving you control.

How to request deletion:

To delete your account and all associated data from Rizzler, please email us at:
support@rizzlerr.app

In your email, include:

  • The email address or username linked to your account
  • A clear request to delete your account and data

We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 days.

What data is deleted:

  • ✅ Account credentials
  • ✅ Chat history with AI bots
  • ✅ Posts from the Advice Wall
  • ✅ Any personal preferences or settings

What data may be retained:

  • ❌ Anonymous analytics (non-identifiable)

What data we do NOT collect:

  • ❌ No financial or payment information is collected or stored by us

All purchases are handled securely through Google Play Billing. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

You can request deletion anytime by emailing us directly.
